Description

Lost Lily is a 2D puzzle platformer about a child’s journey through an ominous forest located in the Interstice, a realm where the world of the living and the spectral collide. You play as an unassuming ghost who acts as the guide for lost souls. Using possession, manipulate the strange environment and its residing horrors to escort the child to safety.

History

Sole Horror, a student team founded at Swinburne University 2022 worked together to develop the Indie puzzle-platform game Lost Lily. Lost Lily began development in early 2022, had a playable by June 2022,which demonstrated the game’s core mechanics and its intended aesthetics and was presented at Swinburne’s 2022 Open Day. Following this, development continued until PAX Melbourne, October 2022, where Lost Lily was once again presented as part of the Swinburne booth and showcased student talent.

Features

  • Solve intricate puzzles! - Put your intelligence to the test and manipulate the environment in shrewd and cunning ways with your powers of possession.
  • A new adventure! - Journey through a visually stunning world and learn the secrets of the Interstice.
  • Lead the way! - Keep Lily safe as you guide her safely through the dangerous forest.
  • Fill out the journal! - Gather flowers and fill out Lily’s journal, learning about the horrors that reside in the Interstice.
